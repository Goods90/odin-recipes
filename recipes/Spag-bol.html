Spaghetti Bolognaise

Rich ragu sauce with minced pork and beef served with al dente spaghetti.

Beautifully colored and rich family feast, sure to be a complete winner in your household.

Ingredients:

  • Olive Oil
  • Spaghetti - 500g
  • Minced Pork and Beef - 500g
  • Large Carrot (diced)- 1
  • Large Onion (diced)- 1
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese - 100g
  • Passata - 1 tin
  • Garlic (crushed)- 3 cloves
  • Beef Stock - 1 cube
  • Basil (Fresh or dried) - 1tsp

Method:

  1. Bring a frying pan to medium heat with a splash of olive oil for frying
  2. Add chopped onion and fry until brown
  3. Add garlic and carrots to the pan
  4. Fry for a further minute then add the minced meat
  5. Brown all over and then add passata and beef stock
  6. Bring whole pan to a boil and let simmer
  7. Bring 2.5L of water to boil in a separate saucepan and add the spaghetti
  8. Once cooked following the package instructions, drain and serve
  9. Add a healthy spoonful of bolognaise mixture ontop of the pasta to serve
  10. Garnish with grated parmesan and enjoy!
