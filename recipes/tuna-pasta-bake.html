Tuna Pasta Bake

Glorious baked cheesy pasta with tuna and sweetcorn in the mix.

This recipe takes no more than 30 minutes to make and will be a firm family favourite. The melted cheese on top combined with the salty Tuna is sure to make the daily menu in your household.

Ingredients:

  • Penne Pasta - 500g
  • Tinned Tuna - 2 tins
  • Sweetcorn - 1 tin
  • Mature Cheddar Cheese (grated) - big handful
  • Milk (preferably full fat) - 100ml
  • Plain Flour - 2 Tbsp
  • Butter - 3 Tbsp

Method:

  1. Bring 2.5L of water to boil in a large saucepan and add pasta
  2. Melt butter in a seperate saucepan
  3. Once melted add plain flour and mix until it becomes a paste
  4. Slowly add milk stiring continuously until it becomes a thick white sauce
  5. add a handful of the grated cheese to the white sauce and stir
  6. Drain pasta once cooked and mix well with white sauce
  7. Drain and add Tuna and Sweetcorn into the saucepan then mix well
  8. Sprinkle the remaining Cheddar over the top and put under the grill for 10 minutes or until cheese is golden and cripy
  9. Season once served and enjoy!
